Bayes Rules! is the textbook of the course. It is freely available at bayesrulesbook.com and you will not need to purchase a hard copy. However, if you prefer a hard-copy it can be pre-ordered on the publisher's website. Note that the hard-copy of the book is not released until late January.
VPN If you are not on campus, you will need to connect through VPN to access resources that are only accessible on campus. Follow the instructions on the link.
R and RStudio We will use the R language which is a popular programming language used by statisticians and data scientists. We will use RStudio interface while using the R language. We will use many R packages but two of the most important are bayesrules and rstan.
zoom All the meetings will take place on zoom. You can only attend meetings if you use a UCI registered email. Make sure your zoom account is through UCI.
Gradescope All your assignments will be on Gradescope. You will receive further instructions on how to navigate using Gradescope.
Canvas We will use Canvas only for keeping track of grades. This is your official gradebook.
ed We will use ed discussion as our online "social" space. You can ask and answer questions, and discuss topics. If you have a question about anything in this course, it is very likely that one of your classmates also has the exact same question. In order to reduce the number of emails, and to form a learning community, we will be using ed. If you need to ask anything personal, please email me. You always have the choice to post anoynmously and your classmates would not know who has written the post but I would know who you are. Before you start writing your post, please make sure to search first if the same question has already been posted. Always follow online communication etiquette when posting on ed.
